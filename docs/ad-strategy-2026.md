# Ad Strategy Reference — Lux Media (2026)

## Meta Ad Creative Benchmarks

### Format Performance (Static Ads)
- **70-80% image / 20-30% text** — optimal ratio
- **Doctor/provider photo** = $0.04-$0.05/interaction (vs $0.15+ stock/no photo)
- **Text overlay under 20%** of image area (Meta penalizes above 20%)
- **4:5 (1080×1350)** for feed placements, **1:1 (1080×1080)** for FB feed
- **High contrast text** — minimum 4.5:1 WCAG ratio

### Hook Hierarchy (by conversion rate)
1. **Question hooks** — "¿Ve borroso?" (lowest CPR historically, $0.38 campaign)
2. **Pain/symptom hooks** — "Las cataratas no duelen. Por eso son tan peligrosas."
3. **Stat hooks** — "99% de los pacientes recuperan su visión"
4. **Authority hooks** — "23+ años de experiencia"

### Copy Frameworks
- **PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solution):** Best for cold traffic. Lead with pain.
- **AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action):** Best for warm/retargeting.
- **Direct (Hook → Body → CTA):** Best for bottom funnel (scarcity, FAQ).

### Copy Length
- **Primary text:** 90-150 chars optimal (mobile visible without "see more")
- **Hook:** Under 50 chars — must stop the scroll
- **CTA:** 3-5 words — action verb + benefit

## Kill/Scale Rules (from CPR data)

| CPR Range | Action | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| < $0.66 | **Scale** — increase budget 20-30% | Don't touch creative |
| $0.67 - $1.00 | **Maintain** — keep running | Monitor frequency |
| $1.01 - $1.50 | **Optimize** — test new hook/image | Give 3-5 days |
| $1.51 - $3.00 | **Review** — likely creative fatigue | New creative or pause |
| > $3.00 | **Pause** — replace immediately | Don't spend more |

## Frequency Rules
- **Frequency < 2.0:** Healthy
- **Frequency 2.0-3.0:** Watch for fatigue, prepare replacements
- **Frequency > 3.0:** Creative fatigue — rotate immediately

## Medical Advertising Guardrails

### Never use:
- "100% seguro" / "Funciona siempre" / "Garantizado" / "Sin ningún riesgo" / "Cura definitiva"

### Qualified alternatives:
- "Procedimiento seguro y efectivo"
- "Alta tasa de éxito"
- "Miles de pacientes satisfechos"
- "Tecnología de última generación"
- "Más de 20 años de experiencia"
